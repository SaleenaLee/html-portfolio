The Best Movies According to Saleena

My top 3 movies of ALL TIME!

Kiki's Delivery Service

One of the first anime's I've ever seen and my favorite! The art style is captivating and nostalgic.

Dazed and Confused

A cult classic that perfectly embodies adolesence, the summer of love, and small town living.

Everything, Everywhere, All at Once

A newer film with an outstanding cast, a very unique storyline, and iconic visuals.
